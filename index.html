Intro

I am an ETH master student who is very passionate about data analysis and artificial intelligence. Specifically, I've worked in the applications of data science in the life science and healthcare industries.

Even though I obtained a bachelor in mechanical engineering, I chose to fully focus in data science and machine learning during my masters. Under the tutoring of Prof. Siegwart, head of the Institute of Robotics and Intelligent Systems at ETH [link lab], I developed my exitement and understanding in a wide range of topics, going from computer vision and image analysis to deep learning.

Thriven by the possibility of applying my knowledge aquired during my studies, I chose to join IBM with their Master@IBM program [link Master@IBM]. During the past year and a half, I've been able to participate in the delivery of consulting projects for the Life Science industry, aquiring my first working experience.

In addition, I've always loved to take part in side activities besides university and work. Until last year I was the president of the Spanish-speaking Student Association of ETH, being able to organize several different events for students and professors.[link facebook lsvz][link iv podium]
